About Us

Located on the picturesque coast of York Beach, Maine, Laird's Lobsters has been a cornerstone of the local seafood scene since 1920. Founded by Laird Masterson Sr., our restaurant began as a humble shack serving fresh lobster to the community. Over the decades, we've grown into a celebrated full-service restaurant known for exceptional seafood, sustainable practices, and a commitment to our local community.

We proudly honor our founder's legacy by combining over a century of tradition with modern culinary innovation to create unforgettable dining experiences.

Why Choose Laird's Lobsters?

  • Sustainability: 10% of our profits go to families in need, and we contribute over 10,000 meals annually to combat child food insecurity.
  • Exceptional Quality: Our seafood is sourced daily from local fisheries to ensure the freshest taste.
  • Heritage and Expertise: With over 100 years of experience, we blend tradition and modern culinary techniques.
  • Community Roots: Our team includes local talent and family members, bringing passion and dedication to every dish.
